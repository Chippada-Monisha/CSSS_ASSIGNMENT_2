Height Comparision in CSS

Height: 2rem (Relative to font-size of the root element)

Height: 2em (Relative to the font-size of the element)

Height: 2vh (Relative to 1% of the height of the viewport*)

Height: 2vw (Relative to 1% of the width of the viewport*)

Height: 5px (Relative to the viewing device)
